## Purity re-audit (R2)

No rendered outcome is computed outside the event log:

- The scene is `projectScene(log, playhead)` — a pure, referentially-stable function
  (`store/select.test.ts`), asserted here to reproduce the engine's grouping counts and
  the exact `found` element rather than re-deriving them.
- The engine kernel imports no React/Next/Zustand/viz (`engine.boundary.test.ts` +
  the ESLint `no-restricted-imports` override), so an outcome *cannot* be invented on
  the render side.
